Sprains & Stains

Sprains involve ligament damage, causing pain and swelling, while strains result from muscle or tendon injury, causing discomfort and restricted movement.

Tennis Elbow

Tennis elbow, or lateral epicondylitis, is an overuse injury causing pain and tenderness on the outer elbow, often aggravated by repetitive arm movements.

ACL Tear

ACL tear is a common sports injury, causing instability, swelling, and pain in the knee, often requiring surgery for full recovery.

Fractures

Fractures are breaks in bones, causing pain, swelling, and sometimes deformity. Treatment varies from immobilization to surgery depending on severity.
